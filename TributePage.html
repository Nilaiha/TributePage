A Tribute to Strawberries

Don't they look delicious?

Here are some interesting facts about Strawberries!

  • A strawberry is not a berry in a botanical sense, because berries have their seeds on the inside
  • In a way, the strawberry is a false fruit, because the actual fruits are the many tiny "seeds" on the outside of the strawberry
  • Therefore, the average strawberry has 200 seeds
  • The strawberry is part of the rose family
  • Eight strawberries have more Vitamin C than an Orange
  • The ancient Romans used strawberries to treat fever, sore throat and even depression
  • The world record for the heaviest strawberries is 250 grams - it had a circumference of nearly 30 cm and was grown in Japan
  • In Belgium, there is a museum dedicated entirely to strawberries
  • Strawberries are typically the first fruit to ripen in spring
  • There are different color variants such as yellow, white, black and purple
